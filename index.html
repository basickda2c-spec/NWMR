Know the Dangers

4 Key Risks of
Untreated Mould

Left unchecked, mould spreads quickly and becomes far more costly to address. Here's what's at stake.

Respiratory Health

Mould spores are airborne and can trigger asthma, allergies, and chronic respiratory conditions — especially dangerous for children and the elderly.

Learn More

Property Damage

Mould degrades plasterwork, timber, and paint. If left for months it can compromise structural integrity, leading to expensive repair bills.

Learn More

Legal Liability

Under Awaab's Law, landlords must address mould and damp within strict timeframes. Failure to comply risks significant fines and legal action.

Learn More

Falling Property Value

Visible mould signals poor maintenance to buyers and surveyors. Untreated mould can directly reduce a property's market value and mortgage eligibility.
